# Conversion Redesign Spec — Client Review Implementation

## Flow (homepage)

1. **Hero** — Outcome-led headline, social proof sub, primary + urgency CTA
2. **Trust block** — 5.0 from 200+ clients, one hero testimonial
3. **Transformations** — Before/after as centerpiece, labels, “View More” CTA
4. **Services strip** — “Services I Specialize In” (Lived-in, Blonding, Extensions, Bridal)
5. **About** — Bold hook, scannable bullets, hobbies lower
6. **Testimonials** — Reinforce trust
7. **Closing CTA** — “Ready to love your hair again?” → Book
8. **Footer**

## CTA strategy

- **Primary:** “Book Appointment” (one label, consistent)
- **Urgency:** “Only 2 Spots Left This Week” (optional; control via constant)
- Sticky header: Book button always visible, visually dominant
- Repeat CTA after every major section

## Hierarchy

- Section contrast (background shifts)
- Headlines much larger
- Paragraph max-width reduced (~55ch)
- More spacing between sections

## Brand (future)

- 70s editorial: warm tan/burnt orange, grain, serif (Playfair/Canela), editorial imagery
- Current: stronger hierarchy and conversion first; visual identity in next pass.
